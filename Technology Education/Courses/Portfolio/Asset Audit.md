### Overview

This is an activity where you will classify the assets of a home network. Taking a complete asset inventory and classifying them based on importance and sensitivity.

### Scenario

An inventory of network devices is key for asset management tools as they can underscore key sensitive assets that require extra attention.

You are opening a small business from home and need to inventory your network devices and which ones contain sensitive information.
